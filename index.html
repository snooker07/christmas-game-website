Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

1. Introduction
Fundelits ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data in the mobile application "Christmas Breaker."

2. Data Collection & Advertising
We use Unity Ads to provide a free-to-play experience. Unity Ads may collect data from your device to serve personalized advertisements. This includes:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA/GAID)
  • IP address
  • Game data (session length, level progress)

You can opt-out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Tracking on iOS). For more details, please review Unity's Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

3. In-App Purchases
All financial transactions (e.g., "Remove Ads") are handled exclusively by Apple via the App Store. We do not process or store any credit card or banking information on our servers.

4. Children's Privacy
Our game is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. We do not request user age, birthdate, or name within the application.
